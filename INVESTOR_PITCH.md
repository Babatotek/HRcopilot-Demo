## THE DIFFERENTIATOR: LEAKAGE INTELLIGENCE

This is HRcopilot's most powerful and defensible feature — and nothing like it exists in the Nigerian or African market.

**Organizational Leakage** is the silent drain on company revenue: ghost workers, overtime fraud, procurement kickbacks, payroll padding, underperforming cost centers. Most companies don't know it's happening.

HRcopilot's **Leakage Intelligence Engine** cross-references HR, payroll, attendance, finance, and procurement data in real time to surface exactly where money is leaking — and by how much. It benchmarks against industry profiles, applies exchange-rate-adjusted calculations, and delivers actionable recommendations to the CEO dashboard.

> For a 500-person Nigerian company, this feature alone can recover ₦50M–₦200M annually. That's an ROI that pays for the platform 10x over.
